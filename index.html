Track 04: The Masks

Hidden Identity

Slipknot is famous for wearing terrifying masks. The lead singer, Corey Taylor, said the masks serve a very specific psychological purpose.

What is that purpose?

Correct. Corey stated: "It's our way of becoming more intimate with the music. It's a way for us to become unconscious of who we are and what we do outside of music." It represents bringing their inner pain and "ugliness" to the surface.

Track 11: Observation

Fact Check

"Slipknot claims they write their aggressive music purely because they hate each other and want to vent their frustrations against the band."

False. The music is a catharsis. It's a way to process personal pain, depression, and anger in a safe environment, turning negative energy into a powerful connection with their fans.
